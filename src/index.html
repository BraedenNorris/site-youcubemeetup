@YouCubeMeetup

With the recent post-poning of Mineorama a group of community members, YouTubers, and server hosts have came together with the intention of making the most of the situation.

YouCube Meetup is the result of a drive to rectify a wrongdoing in the community. On July 12th from 9AM to 6PM we'll be hosting a fan meetup with many who originally intended to attend Mineorama. No charge. No catch. Open to everyone. We're finalizing information on the venue, permits, and other information and we'll announce details on Twitter as it becomes available!


YouCube Meetup is not affiliated with Mojang AB, YouTube, Mineorama, nor is it endorsed by any of those entities. YouCube Meetup is comprised of volunteer, unpaid staff. Images on this site are used with the permission of their respective ownwers.
